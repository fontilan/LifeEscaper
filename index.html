How it works

Feeling overwhelmed? Life is getting crazy and hectic? You wish you could just drop everything and focus on yourself for a change? Look no further! Our team will help you restore your peace of mind and a sense of balance.


Our hand-crafted plans (“Camps”) are designed to bring peace and serenity back into your life. Be it through yoga, meditation or music – after just a few days you will feel energized and ready to tackle the world head on.

Yoga Camp

This ancient art of harmonious movement is the perfect way to reestablish harmony in your busy life. With the help of our professional yogis you will develop strength and flexibility which you can then utilize in every situation.


For our beginners we offer introductory classes teaching you the proper techniques of breathing and movement to establish a strong foundation for the more advanced techniques. No experience is necessary and there is no age restriction – yoga can be learned easily by anyone.


For our more advanced users we offer more challenging sessions with some of the best yogis in the world. With their help you will achieve mastery over your body and mind.

Meditation Camp

The art of meditation is deceptively simple – it’s just sitting. Anyone who tried to do it though will know that this simplicity is complicated by out mind’s tendency to wander. No worries! With the help of our seasoned meditation masters you will learn to overcome your “monkey mind” and find peace and serenity.


For beginners we offer introductory classes which are shorter and more playful, teaching the basics of breath-work and concentration.


For more experienced meditators we offer deep focus sessions, lasting up to 4 hours, allowing the practitioner to reach the state of pure being.

Sounds amazing!

Music Camp

Your heart is singing, yet you almost forgot its tune – it's time to return to the wonderful music of your soul. With the help of our professional musicians, musicologist, and music therapists you will rediscover the harmony that drives your life.


With a selection of the best hand-crafted instruments (guitars, drums, flutes, shakers, saxophones etc.) you will discover the wonderful world of sound and learns its healing power.


And if you cannot play any instrument? Worry not! Not only will you be surrounded by professional musicians who will help you pick up the basics in no time, but you can also engage in “free music” – a spontaneous improvisation coming straight from your heart. There are no wrong notes there!
